How to Play Sudoku

1

The Goal

Fill the 9√ó9 grid with numbers 1-9 so that each row, column, and 3√ó3 box contains all digits from 1 to 9 without repetition.

2

Getting Started

Look for rows, columns, or boxes that already have many numbers filled in. These are often the easiest places to find your next move.

3

Using Notes

Use the Notes mode to mark possible candidates for each cell. This helps you keep track of your logical deductions.

4

Advanced Techniques

Learn techniques like "Naked Pairs", "Hidden Singles", and "X-Wing" to solve harder puzzles efficiently.

Pro Tips

  • Start with the easiest cells and work your way up
  • Use the process of elimination systematically
  • Don't guess - every move should be logical
  • Practice regularly to improve your skills
